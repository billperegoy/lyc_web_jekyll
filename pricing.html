Our number one goal at Lawrence Yoga Collective is to make yoga accessible to everyone. Given that, all classes are offered on a pay as you can, donation basis. We do provide three suggested levels. For all payment level, we accept cash, checks or credit cards.

Summer Special

We are running a summer special for unlimited yoga all summer long for just $150. We know it’s harder to get into the studio during the busy summer days, so we want to make it as inexpensive and easy as possible to keep your practice going. You can pay in person at the studio or buy online using the form at bottom of this page.
